How do I reset my password?
To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address and follow the instructions in the email we send you.

How do I update my billing information?
You can update your billing information by going to the "Account Settings" section and selecting "Payment Methods." From there, you can edit, remove, or add a new credit card or billing address.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, you may cancel your subscription at any time from the "Billing" section of your account dashboard. Once canceled, your access will continue until the end of your billing period.

What is your refund policy?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all new customers. If you're not satisfied with the service, please contact support within 30 days of your purchase to request a refund.

How long does it take to receive a refund?
Refunds are typically processed within 5–7 business days after approval. The actual time may vary depending on your bank or credit card provider.

Do you offer phone support?
At this time, we offer support via email and live chat only. You can reach out to our team through the “Help” section of your account for immediate assistance.

Where can I find my invoices?
Invoices are available in the "Billing History" section of your account. You can download PDF copies for your records at any time.

How do I change my email address?
To change your email address, navigate to "Profile Settings" and select "Update Email." You may be asked to re-authenticate before the change is applied.

Is my data secure?
Yes, we take data security very seriously. All user data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and we comply with major data protection regulations including GDPR and CCPA.
